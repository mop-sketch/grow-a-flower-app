🌸 Grow a Flower — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile game Grow a Flower (the “App”) handles your information. Your privacy matters to us, and we have built the App to need as little of your data as possible — in fact, none.

The short version

Grow a Flower does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The game runs entirely on your device and works fully offline. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.

Information we collect

None. The App does not ask for, gather, or send any personal data such as your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers. Your game progress and settings stay on your device.

Internet and network access

The App can function completely offline. It uses network-related permissions for two limited, non-data-collecting reasons:

  • Connectivity status — the App checks whether your device currently has an internet connection so it can enable or disable an optional link to an external website. No information about you is read or sent.
  • Optional external link — if you choose to tap the “Apple Picker” link, your device’s browser opens an external web page. That separate website is not controlled by us and has its own privacy practices. The App itself does not send any of your data.

Third-party services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services. No data is shared with anyone because no data is collected.

Children’s privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Because no personal data is collected at all, the App does not gather data from children under any applicable age threshold.
