About Grow a Plant.CO

Why we made Grow a flower?

Grow a flower is a game where you can nurture and grow a virtual flower. The goal is to help your flower thrive and reach its full potential. The game is built to show the diffculties of growing a flower, and the importance of consistent care and attention in order to achieve success.

Who are we?

We are a company dedicated to nurturing plants, landscapeing and promoting environmental awareness.

Our Mission

Our mission is to plant flowers and contribute to a greener and more sustainable environment, and every flower grown in the game represents a flower planted in nature.
